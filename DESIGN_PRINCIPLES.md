# Design Principles

1. Conclusions before charts.
2. Progressive disclosure.
3. Every page answers one question.
4. Scan mode and Investigation mode.
5. Feature is the primary object.
6. Representation-first, architecture-second.
7. Local-first execution.
8. CLI and TUI are clients, not architecture roots.
9. Typed domain objects over generic dictionaries.
10. Stream large activation datasets.
11. TUI polish and analysis correctness must evolve independently.
